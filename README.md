Problem Statement :Sentiment analysis of Amazon kindle store reviews.
1) This project is to perform a supervised sentiment analysis on the review data that from Amazon kindle store.
2) The data consists of 982,619 reviews spanning May 1996 to July 2014.
3) Reviews with overall rating of 1, 2, or 3 are labelled as negative ("neg"/1), and reviews with overall rating of 4 or 5 are labelled as positive ("pos"/0).
4) Dataset is available here: http://jmcauley.ucsd.edu/data/amazon/
